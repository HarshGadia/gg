Export & Bulk

Global Containerized Logistics and Premium Packaging Standards

Seaworthy Shipments

Quarry to Port Logistics

Gadia Granites operates direct logistics pipelines from our Jaipur godowns and quarry sites in Rajasthan to the major sea ports of Western India. We regularly service bulk specifications for architects, distributors, and large-scale builders globally.

Loading Ports

Mundra Port, Gujarat (nearest, ~650 km) or Kandla Port, Gujarat (~700 km). Both offer high FCL frequency.

Packaging Specs

Vertical wooden A-frames for slabs; fumigated, heavy-duty seaworthy wooden boxes for tiles, lined with styrofoam sheets.
